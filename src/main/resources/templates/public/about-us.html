About Us

Know more about the team behind TravelWith

Hello! I'm Vishal Jadhav, a first-year MCA student and the team leader of this project. This is our college mini-project developed during the first year with a group of five enthusiastic members.

Our project, TravelWith, is a bus seat booking web application aimed at simplifying the travel booking experience. Along with the seat reservation feature, we also plan to extend our project in the future to include exciting trip packages and hotel bookings.
